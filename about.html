- About Us -

Artsify is an online gallery and marketplace that serves as a platform to optimize the circulation and engagement of art.

- Our Mission-

To allow its artists to focus on creating work while we assist them in the selling, marketing, and distribution of their pieces.

- And More -

To connect artists who want to make their artwork more accessible to nonprofit or community service organizations.
